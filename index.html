About

Nick is a data analyst working in the transportation industry, with a background in auditing and the financial division. Also communicating directly with clients and other departments to resolve complex problems has promoted not only a customer first approach but also working in a team environment.


As an analyst with adhoc reporting duties to the officer group, he has gained a deep understanding of seeking the reason behind the questions being asked. This not only aids in answer the question being asked but the one that isn't being asked. With experience in Tableau, PowerBI, and SSMS he has been a key asset in restructuring operational reporting across the company.
